One city, twenty-plus districts, one crew

Winnipeg's pipes tell the story of the city it's built on. Mature elms send roots into clay-tile laterals in the older districts; the newer subdivisions run modern PVC. Wherever you are, we diagnose with a camera before we quote — and we get there same day. Jump to a district:

Central

Downtown & the inner core

The city's oldest housing stock — and its oldest pipes. Roots and clay-tile joints are the usual story here.

Wolseley
Clay tile · roots

Century-old character homes shaded by mature elms — prime territory for root intrusion and offset joints in original clay-tile sewers. We camera-scope before we ever cut.

West End
Grease · roots

Dense, older housing with aging and sometimes shared service connections. Frequent grease and root blockages — hydro jetting clears them properly.

Downtown
Commercial · cast iron

Mixed residential and commercial lines, much of it original cast iron. We handle scale buildup, recurring backups, and after-hours commercial work.

Crescentwood
Cast iron · scale

Grand older homes built on cast-iron risers. Slow drains here are usually scale and corrosion — we descale and inspect rather than guess.

West

River Heights & the west side

Mature tree canopy plus larger lots — a mix of aging clay and newer laterals depending on the street.

River Heights
Clay tile · roots

Towering elms mean aggressive roots in aging clay laterals — one of our busiest districts. Hydro jetting plus annual maintenance keeps these lines flowing.

Tuxedo
Large lots · mixed

Bigger lots mean longer runs to the main, so blockages hide farther out. Camera inspection pinpoints the spot before any digging is considered.

Charleswood
Mixed era · clay/PVC

A true mixed bag — older streets on clay tile, newer pockets on PVC. We diagnose what you've actually got before quoting a thing.

St. James-Assiniboia
Post-war · grease

Post-war housing near the airport, mostly cast iron and clay. Kitchen-line grease is the recurring culprit — we clear it and keep it clear.

North

Seven Oaks, Kildonan & the north end

Everything from historic Point Douglas to modern Maples builds — we cover the full range.

Seven Oaks
Mixed era

Established streets beside newer development. Whatever's in the ground — clay tile or modern PVC — we've worked it and we'll scope it.

The Maples
Newer · PVC

Largely newer PVC laterals, so root issues are rarer — but settling and construction debris still cause backups. We jet and camera as needed.

North & East Kildonan
Riverbank · settling

Riverbank settling can crack and belly older lines along the Red. We recommend a camera inspection to catch problems before they back up.

Point Douglas
Historic · cast iron

Some of the oldest infrastructure in the city. Original cast iron and clay tile — we handle the recurring blockages these vintage lines bring.

East

St. Boniface, Transcona & the east

Historic French Quarter homes through to brand-new Sage Creek subdivisions.

St. Boniface
Historic · mixed

Historic French Quarter homes alongside newer infill. Older cast-iron stacks and clay laterals are common — we scope first, then quote a flat price.

Transcona
Clay tile · roots

An established rail-town neighbourhood where clay tile and tree roots are the usual culprits. Hydro jetting restores full flow on overgrown lines.

Windsor Park
Post-war · clay

Tidy post-war bungalows on aging clay laterals. Backwater-valve checks matter in the pockets that sit closer to the river.

Sage Creek
Newer · PVC

New PVC laterals mean fewer root problems — but improper installs and construction debris still cause early backups. We camera-verify and clear.

South

St. Vital, Fort Garry & the south

Low-lying riverside pockets where backwater valves and clay-tile settling are worth watching.

St. Vital
Clay tile · backwater

Post-war bungalows on clay tile, plus newer builds toward the river. In the low-lying pockets, backwater-valve checks are well worth doing.

Norwood
Riverside · bellies

Riverside lots where clay tile is prone to settling. A camera inspection finds bellies and cracks early — before they turn into a backup.

Fort Garry
University · rentals

University-area homes and rentals see heavy use. We clear student-house clogs fast and inspect aging laterals for landlords and owners alike.

Bridgwater & Waverley West
New build · PVC

The city's newest subdivisions, on modern PVC. Problems here are usually install-related — we camera-verify and clear construction debris.

Beyond the Perimeter

The surrounding communities

Just outside the city limits — a septic-to-municipal mix. We service both.

Headingley
Septic + municipal

A mix of municipal and septic systems west of the city. We clear lines, jet, and inspect for both — just give us the lay of the land when you call.

East St. Paul
Acreage · mixed

Larger acreage properties just past the northeast Perimeter. Longer laterals and mixed systems — camera inspection earns its keep out here.

West St. Paul
Growing · mixed

A fast-growing community north of the city on a mix of older and new lines. Same trucks, same same-day service, just a short drive further.
