Do you need us?

When to call

Not every slow drain needs a plumber today, but some do. A sink that drains slow once a month after a shave is usually hair you can pull with a zip tool from the hardware store. Try that first.

The drains that warrant a same-day call: a tub holding water when you shower, a kitchen sink backing up when the dishwasher runs, a laundry standpipe overflowing onto the floor, a basement floor drain gurgling when other fixtures run, more than one drain slow at the same time.

The last one is the big tell. Multiple slow fixtures usually means the problem isn't in any one drain. It's in the sewer line downstream of all of them. That's a different job, different equipment, different page.

If more than one drain is slow, the issue is probably in your sewer line.
