Who we are

Sewer and drain.
That's the whole business.

We started Winnipeg Sewer & Drain in 2015 for a simple reason: when your basement is filling with sewage on a Sunday night, you don't want a general handyman who does a bit of everything. You want someone who clears sewer lines all day, every day, and has seen your exact problem a hundred times.

So that's all we do. From our shop on Main Street in the North End, our trucks run drain cleaning, sewer line cleaning, hydro jetting, camera inspections, and frozen-line thawing across every Winnipeg neighbourhood. We don't install water heaters or remodel bathrooms — and when a call isn't a drain or sewer issue, we'll point you to a plumbing partner we trust instead of pretending it's our job.

Specializing changes how the work goes. Our techs know which Wolseley and River Heights homes still run clay tile, which Crescentwood blocks hide cast iron, and where the roots come back. That experience is why most jobs get diagnosed and cleared in a single visit.

How we work

Five promises we actually keep.

None of this is a slogan. It's the stuff Winnipeg homeowners keep writing about in their reviews — so it's the stuff we hold ourselves to.

A person answers.

Call our line and a real human picks up — often the same day, often within minutes — and gives you a straight time window. No phone tree, no callback roulette.

Quote first.

You get a price over the phone before a truck rolls. If we find something bigger on site, we show you and re-quote before doing the work — never after.

We show up — fast.

Active backups get same-day service, weekends and holidays included. Emergency arrivals routinely land in 15 to 30 minutes inside the city.

We explain what we find.

Our techs walk you through what's actually wrong, in plain language, and put a camera down the line when it earns its place — so you're not guessing.

We leave it clean.

Reviewers mention it more than anything else: we tidy up, move what we moved back, and leave your space the way we found it.

Fair, honest pricing.

No weekend surcharge, no upsell theatre. The most common line in our reviews after a name is “reasonable” and “fair.”
