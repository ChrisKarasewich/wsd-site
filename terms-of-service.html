2. Our services

WSD provides residential and commercial sewer and drain services in Winnipeg and surrounding areas, including drain cleaning, sewer line cleaning, hydro jetting, sewer camera inspection, and frozen sewer thawing.

  • We assess and service the lines and fixtures described in your work order. We do not perform work outside that scope without your approval.
  • Sewer and drain systems are often aged, hidden, or shared. Some conditions can't be known until a line is opened, cleared, or scoped, and results can vary with the condition of your pipes.
  • Where a line is too damaged, collapsed, or obstructed to clear safely, we'll tell you and recommend next steps rather than risk further damage.

We stand behind our work. The specific warranty for your job \u2014 what's covered and for how long \u2014 is set out on your invoice or work order. In general:

  • Clearing a drain restores flow; it does not repair underlying pipe damage. Where we recommend a camera inspection or repair and it's declined, recurring issues on that line aren't covered.
